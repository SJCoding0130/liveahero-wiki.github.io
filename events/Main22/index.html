Campaign: Main Quest Book 2 Chapter 2

Main Quest Book 2 Chapter 2
Duration
Gacha7 Sep 2023 20:00 JST to 28 Sep 2023 14:00 JST
News link

The main quests of Book 2 chapter 2 has been released.

In commemoration of the new chapter release, a limited time gacha summon is also released which include Exio (Hero), Danzo (Hero and Sidekick), Galvo (Hero and Sidekick) , Grigory (Hero and Sidekick).

For the period of Main Quest Book 2 Chapter 2, there will be half stamina cost for every episode in Main Quest.
