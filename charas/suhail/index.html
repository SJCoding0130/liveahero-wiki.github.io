Personal Data 1

ヴィラン「キャプテン・トロネシオン」へと変身して戦闘に臨む彼の主力装備は、左腕の義手である。この腕は機械惑星トロネシオン出身の「友」が遺したパーツを移植したもので、対カイブツ戦用に仕込まれたシステムにより、スハイルの意志に感応して様々な形態に変形させることが出来る。お気に入りの形態は、対象目掛けてどこまでも伸びる「マジックハンド」と、「エネルギーガン」。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

He transform into the villain “Captain Tronesion” to fight, with an artificial left arm. This arm was transplanted with parts left behind by a “friend” from the mechanical planet Tronesion. With a system built for fighting against kaibutsu, it can transform into various forms in response to Suhail’s will. His favorite forms are the Magic Hand, which can extends endlessly towards its target, and the Energy Gun.

Personal Data 2

彼にとって左腕は、かつて共に歩みながらも先に散っていた「友」の形見であり、「宇宙一の海賊になる」という誓いの証でもある。何度逮捕されようと脱獄し、どんなにマークされようと団の先頭に立って現場に出張って豪快に笑う彼の破天荒な生き様の裏には、かつて「友」と交わした約束を果たす為に邁進し続けるという、彼なりの友情に対する誠実さが存在するのだ。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

For him, his left arm is a memento of a “friend” he once went through together but had disappeared. It’s also a testament to his vow to “be the best pirate in the universe”. No matter how many times he was arrested, he always escaped from prison. No matter how he was marked, he would stand at the front of his group and laugh boldly at the scene, but behind his unconventional way of life, He keeps striving to fulfill the promise he once made to his “friend”, he is very sincere when it comes to friendships.

Personal Data 1

宇宙一の海賊になる、そう決意したスハイルがトロネシオンに降り立った日、スハイルは一人の無機生命体と遭遇し、それが唯一無二の「友」との出会いとなった――過去の記憶が呼び覚まされ、それに応えるように友から託された左腕のパーツが拡声器のフォルムに変化した。応援団を彷彿とさせるスーツはパラレルウェポンと相互作用を起こし、どこまでも遠く響く声となる。友とスハイルの重なった声は星をも揺らし、聞く者の注目を全て掻っ攫っていく。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

The day Suhail landed on Tronesion, determined to become the best pirate in the universe, he encountered an inorganic life form, and that was the day he met his one and only friend.
Memories of the past were awakened, and in response, the parts of his left arm entrusted to him by his friend transformed into the form of a loudspeaker.
The suit, reminiscent of a cheering squad, interacted with the parallel weapon and became a voice that echoed far and wide.
The overlapping voices of the friends and Suhail shake the stars and sweep all the attention of the listeners.

Personal Data 2

カイブツに襲われ記憶の一部を失ったスハイルは、失ったものを思い出そうと記憶を辿る。宇宙一の海賊になる為に友と交わした「全宇宙の注目、思慕、富を掻っ攫う」という約束。友と出会った日、海賊団を結成した日、左腕を失った日、……いかなる時も忘れた事はない。その約束があるから、スハイルは豪快に笑っていられる。吹き飛ばす程の熱い応援を全宇宙に届ければ、それが共鳴して返ってきては自身をより駆り立てることを、友から教えてもらったのだから。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

Suhail, who lost part of his memory after being attacked by a Kaibutsu, goes back to his memory to try to remember what he lost.
He exchanged a promise with his friend, to become the best pirate in the universe, to steal all the attention, love and wealth.
He didn’t forget…the day he met his friend, the day he made the pirate gang, the day he lost his left arm
It’s because of this promise that Suhail can laugh heartily.
If you encourage the universe with all your might, then the universe will resonate and also encourage you back, this is what he learned from his friend.

Side Story

７つの恒星系を股にかける、左腕の義手がトレードマークである宇宙海賊団の団長。悪徳な富豪や支配者をターゲットとして襲い、金品を貧しい人々にばら撒く義賊である。あまり頭は良くないが、豪快な性格と誰も彼もが惚れ込むという漢気で、数百人の手下を従えている。「面白い」という感性を何より貴び、面白いと感じたターゲットやヒーローしか相手にしないという、一種の享楽主義者である。

Side Story (Translated)

Sailing across the seven star systems, he is the leader of the space pirates, whose trademark is his artificial left arm. He is a chivalrous thief who steals money and goods from corrupt millionaires and rulers in order to give to the poor. Although not very intelligent, he is a man among men who possesses a big hearted personality capable of charming just about anybody. He is a hedonist who values the concept of “interesting” above all things, and deals only with targets and heroes that he deems as such. Despite not being registered as a hero and being classified as a villain capable of transforming, if there is an “interesting” mission on the table, he reaches out to Hero offices every now and then.

Notes

  • His Full name is Suhail Bautista (スハイル バウティスタ)
  • Suhail is the Lambda star in Constellation Vela (latin for the sails of a ship), It bore the traditional Arabic name السهيل الوزن suhayl al-wazn (Al Suhail al Wazn), but as a modern navigation star this was shortened to Suhail.
  • His skill 1, Murchison meteo borrows its name from a famous meteorite Murchison Meteorite that fell in Australia in 1969 near Murchison, Victoria.
  • He refers to Hydoor in his relation line
