Personal Data 1

故郷で神木に祈りを捧げる為に舞っていた頃に纏っていた衣装を模した装束を纏い、踊り子のヒーローとして戦場に立つ。しかしながら彼は争いごとを好まない、徹底した平和主義者である為に、自身から相手に危害を加えることは稀。彼のヒーローとしての真価は、体表より生み出される日光の恵みの結晶にある。舞い踊る事によって光の結晶は宙に飛び散り、光を浴びた者の傷を癒す効果を発揮するのだ。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Gomeisa wears a costume that resembles the costume he wore when he prayed to the sacred tree in his home town, and fights on the battlefield as a dancer hero. However, due to him being a pacifist who hates hurting others, he rarely ever does his opponent any harm. His value comes from the healing crystals made of pure sunlight that are produced on the surface of his body. Through dance, he is able to scatter those crystals around, and any allies that are exposed to said light will be healed.

Personal Data 2

争いが嫌いで平和主義、可愛いもの好きで誰とでも仲良くしたがる彼を戦場へと赴かせたのは、彼自身が持つ「皆で仲良く踊って遊んで暮らしたい」という願いであった。どんな極悪人でも、例えカイブツでも、いつか分かり合って仲良くする事が出来ると信じる彼は、いつ叶うともしれない願いを叶えるために、今日も戦場で平和を願う舞いを楽しそうに踊るのである。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

He hates disputes, loves cute things and wants to get along with everyone. He has the desire to be friends with anyone, dance and play with them, which is why he chose the path that he is on, going to the battlefield for that reason only. He believes that any villain, even Kaibutsu can one day get to know each other and get along with everyone. He dances on the battlefield for peace, hoping that one day that desire will be fulfilled.

Personal Data 1

クリスマスは、大人も子供も皆が笑顔で、楽しく過ごすものであってほしい。ましてや、そのお祭りが何者かに脅かされて中止になるなんて、そんな悲しいことはあってはならない。そう思うゴメイサが、過去の思い出から得た新たなヒーロー姿。手のひらから、足元から築かれる氷壁は、包み込んで皆を守るため。絶やさぬ笑顔と艶やかな舞いは、楽しい祝祭の日を彩るため。祝福を受けし踊り子たる彼の願いは、きっと見るものをも幸せにすることだろう。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

I want everyone, adults and children alike, to enjoy Christmas with a smile on their faces.
Its too sad that the festival is being threatened by someone and canceled!
Thinking this, Gomeisa has a new hero form obtained from his past memories.
From his palms and feet, the ice wall builds up to envelop and protect everyone.
The shining smile ever etched upon his face and charming dances are meant to brighten up the day of the festival.
As a blessed dancer, his wish is sure to make those who see him happy.

Personal Data 2

かつて故郷で見た、信奉する御神木が自身を凍てつく冬から守るために、自らにあえて氷を纏う姿。そして、自身を元気づけるためにプロキーが持ちかけ、ともに作ったあの雪のクリスマスの日のかまくら。それらはゴメイサに、氷雪に包まれた内側の暖かさを教えてくれた。故郷で得た知見、そしてクリスマスにまつわる暖かな思い出が、ゴメイサの思いを強くし、新たな姿を形作るまでに至ったのだ。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

The sacred tree in his homeland one winter day had become frozen in a protective layer of ice in order to protect itself from the freezing winter wind.
The snowy Christmas Day Kamakura, which Procy had offered to make with him to cheer him up.
These things taught Gomeisa about the inner warmth that can be found within the ice and snow.
The knowledge he gained in his homeland and the warm memories of Christmas strengthened Gomeisa’s feelings and led him to this new form.

Side Story

プロキーが切り盛りする、ヒーロー達の集う酒場でウェイターを務める巨人型宇宙人の青年。巨木と共に息づく文明を持つ惑星の出身であり、巨木信仰の下、神体に祈りを捧げるための踊り子であったため、踊る事を好む。純粋無垢で天真爛漫、人懐こくて他者を疑う事を知らない。身体を触られる事、服を脱ぐ事に抵抗が無く、また体の大きさ故のドジを良く起こす為、プロキーは非常に手を焼いている。

Side Story (Translated)

Gomeisa is a young giant alien working as a waiter in a place where heroes gather under Procy’s management. He is originally from a planet with a civilization that lives with giant trees. He loves dancing and praying to those very same trees and the gods above them. He is innocent, kind, naive and very unsuspecting of others. Having no qualm with being touched, showing no hesitation when asked to undress, causing tons of clumsy accidents due to his massive body; Procy’s just at a loss with him.
