Personal Data 1

敵からは恐れられ、味方からは信頼されていた軍人時代。それはヨシオリにとって、最も他者から自身という存在を認識されていた時期でもあった。「自分という存在を取り戻す」ことを望む彼が変身するのは、そんな頃の記憶を再現したような武装を纏ったヴィランである。カイブツに消化された右腕と腹部を擬似的に取り戻した彼は、異空間へと繋がる腹から、自らが扱ったことのある様々な武器を取り出すことが出来る。お気に入りの武器は機関銃らしい。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

When he was a soldier, he was feared by his enemies and revered by his allies.

This was a time when Yoshiori’s existence was most recognized by others.

He wants to “reclaim his existence,” and as such he transforms into a villain clad in armaments that reflects his memories of that time. He has regained his right arm and abdomen, which were digested by Kaibutsu,

and from his abdomen, which is connected to another space, he can take out various weapons that he has handled before.

His favorite weapon seems to be a machine gun.

Personal Data 2

ある日戦場でカイブツに食われ、消化されかけの状態で吐き出されたヨシオリは「元から存在しなかった人間」と化してしまった。親兄弟や友人の記憶から、戸籍や記録上から、彼という人間は消えてしまったのだ。人々はヨシオリを見ることも触ることも、声を聞くこともできない。絶望的な孤独の中ヨシオリは、どんな手段を使ってでも、自分という存在を取り戻すことを誓う。再びあの愛すべき人々に、自分という存在を認識してもらうため、彼は今日も戦うのだ。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

One day on the battlefield, Yoshiori was eaten by Kaibutsu and was freed in a partially digested state, turning him into “a person who never existed”.

He disappeared from the memories of his parents, siblings, and friends, and from his family register and records.

Normal people can’t see him, touch him, or hear his voice.

In his desperate loneliness, Yoshiori vows to reclaim his existence by any means necessary.

Today, he fights to have his existence recognized by the people he loves again.

Personal Data 1

豪華銀河鉄道のミステリートレイン、その旅路で新たな姿を得たヨシオリ。そのパラレルウェポンは、乗客たちを護り抜く為の二丁拳銃と周囲の危険を察知し映し出すサングラスへと変化し、力を以て脅威を排除するというヨシオリのスタイルを保持しつつ、防衛への信念を表している。また、機動性の高いヒーロースーツは急襲にも適応し、強靱な体力と精神力でターゲットをその身に代えても護り抜く。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Yoshiori gained a new look on his journey on the luxurious galactic mystery train.

His parallel weapon is transformed into twin revolver which is used to protect the passenger, and a sunglasses with the capability to detect and project any possible danger in the surroundings, It represent a belief in protection while still maintaining Yoshiori’s style in eliminating his enemies.

The high mobility of the hero suit is adapted for assault, while the though physical and mental endurance allowed him to protect his target and sacrifice himself if necessary.

Personal Data 2

ヒトから認識をされず、自身をまるで幽霊のようだと感じることのあったヨシオリ。子供の頃に描いた夢、それを叶える為に必死になって訓練に明け暮れていた日々――もう叶うことは無いだろうと斜に構えていた言い訳を全て捨て、彼は再びその手で誰かを護り抜くことを願う。警護のプロであるSPとして、消えかけたその腕でも守れるヒトがいることを信じ、彼はその身を挺して脅威の前に飛び出していく。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

Being unrecognized for most people, Yoshiori sometimes feel like as if he’s a ghost.

The dreams he hold when he’s still a child, the days when he trains himself desperately all the time to reach that…

that kind of dreams will never come true now, was what he used as an excuse, but now he threw it away and wish for once more to be able to protect someone.

As a security professional (SP), even with his vanished arm, he will still appear in front of any threat and put his life on the line against it.

Side Story

オブザーバー・エクシオのボディガードを務める、地球人男性のヴィラン。元は地球圏連合軍の特殊作戦部隊に所属する優秀な軍人であった。カイブツに消化されかけた状態でこちらの世界に戻ってきた不安定な存在であるため、ヒーロー適性を持つ者やオブザーバー以外の生命体からは感知することが出来ない。見た目に違わず粗暴な性格だが、自らが認めた存在に対してはトコトン従順になる、忠犬気質の持ち主でもある。エクシオに対し、並々ならぬ恩義を感じている。

Side Story (Translated)

An earthling villain whose duty is to act as the bodyguard of Exio.

He originally used to be an excellent soldier of the special ops unit of the United Earth’s forces.

After having been almost digested by kaibutsu however, he can only be perceived by heroes and observers.

Despite a wild personality like his appearance suggest, towards those he approve of, he possess doglike loyalty and is thoroughly obedient.

He feels a strong debt of gratitude towards Exio.
