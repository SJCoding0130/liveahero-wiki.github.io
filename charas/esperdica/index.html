Personal Data 1

コーヒーロースター型のパラレルウェポンからは感情を揺さぶる香りを発生させ、嗅いだ相手の行動に影響を与える。例えば香りを充満させることで、敵の行動を鈍らせる、味方の士気を上げるといった使い方も可能。またエスペディカは香りを纏った煙に幻影を投影し、鍛えた体術を以て攻撃する方法を得意な戦法としている。しかし、香りという特性上、嗅覚の無い敵や風の強い場所での戦闘では不利となる。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

The coffee roaster parallel weapon produces an emotionally-charged aroma that affects the behavior of the person who smells it.

For example, it can be used to slow down enemy actions or raise the morale of allies by filling the air with a fragrance.

Esperdica also has a method of projecting illusions in the scented smoke and attacking with his well-trained body techniques.

However, due to this aroma characteristics, he is at a disadvantage when fighting enemies without a sense of smell or in windy locations.

Personal Data 2

幼少期に飲んだコーヒーの味に魅了され、自らの喫茶店を持つために勉強を重ねてきたエスペディカ。何よりその香りを好んでおり、彼はより多くの人がこの香りで優しい気持ちになってほしいと一杯一杯丁寧にコーヒーを淹れる。しかし、彼が今そう心から願えるのは将来の道を選択する岐路に立った時、「安定に留まらず欲張りに全て諦めない」と決断する強さを一人の少年に分けてもらえたからなのだろう。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

Fascinated by the taste of coffee he drank as a child, Esperdica has been studying since then to have his own coffee shop.

He loves the aroma more than anything else, and he brews each cup of coffee with great care, hoping that more people will feel the gentle fragrance of this aroma.

But, he is now at the crossroad of choice for the future he sincerely wish for,

Perhaps it is because one boy shared with him the strength to make decisions, Let go of stability, risk everything greedily.

Side Story

オリエントシティ郊外の閑静な都市公園近くに自身の店を持つ喫茶店のオーナー。店の経営を行いながらバリスタとしても活躍しており、コーヒーに関する深い知識と様々な資格を活かして鑑定士として他店舗等への依頼にも精力的に協力している。なぜか非常に情報通であり、その理由を尋ねても笑って煙に巻かれてしまう。物腰柔らかで穏やかな性格だが、和やかな時間を奪い去る相手には容赦がなく、怒らせると手が付けられないという噂も……

Side Story (Translated)

Owner of a coffee shop with his own store near a quiet urban park in the suburbs of Orient City.

While managing the store, he is also active as a barista, and actively cooperates with other stores as an appraiser, making use of his in-depth knowledge of coffee and various qualifications.

For some reason, he is very well-informed, and when someone ask him why, he laughs and put some smokescreen around that topic.

He is soft-spoken and gentle, but has no mercy on those who steal away his peaceful moments, and is rumored to be unruly when angered….
